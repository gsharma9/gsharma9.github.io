Hedron - Level Designer

.

SUMMARY

.

Hedron is a 3rd person reaction based game in which the player navigates around the cylinder's interior surface and must make their way as fast as possible to the end of the tube, dodging incoming obstacles.

Features include Audio experience with reacting music, high score, various obstacles, tube undulation.

MY ROLES

.

As the Level Designer I tried to mix up patterns that
the player faces within the game. For example
when a player encounters a set of oncoming
obstacles and understands how to maneuver through the obstacles, the frequency of the obstacles start to increase.

Finally I made the level pacing increase in difficulty but towards the end of the game the difficulty decreases as the excitement level continues to incline.
