BATTLE FOR SLUGTERRA - Game and Level Designer

.

SUMMARY

.

Help Eli Shane, to win the battle for Slugterra and
become the best slug slinger in this superb and huge platform game.

Accompanied initially only by your trusty Burpy,
you'll have to find other slugs to have all the skills necessary to pass all levels.

In short, this game will challenge you for many hours of play and fun.

MY ROLES

.

Level Design - Designed multiple levels and
designed enemies to suit the environments.

Game Design - Designed multiple new mechanics
which are slugs for the gameplay. Tuned and
balanced level progression. Created the core gameplay mechanics.

Scripting - Used OGMO and flash scripting tools to create levels and gameplay.

Art and Sound Implementation - Responsible for adding in art and music within the game.
