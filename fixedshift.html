FIXED SHIFT - Game and Level Designer

.

SUMMARY

.

Fixed Shift is a 2D side scrolling puzzle adventure in which the goal is to get to one of the four end portals. There are different items that are required in order to complete the game.

Features include Classic environments and unique puzzles, Rotate the world 90 degrees and Use weapons and grenades.

MY ROLES

.

I made sure that there are items are placed in different sections of the map and the player needs to rotate to those sections in order to get the items.

For example, the player comes across a wall that needs to be blown up. The player would need to look for grenades which are located on another 2D side and then come back to the area to destroy the wall.

I created Fixed Shift in less than two months as a level designer at VFS. The prototyping and puzzle building was done in editor. The theme of the game varies from Mario, Sonic, Megaman and Donkey Kong.
