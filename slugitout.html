SLUG IT OUT! - Game and Level Designer

.

SUMMARY

.

Slug it out! and become the best slugslinger of all time! Play as hero Eli Shane in this fast-paced action puzzle game based on the popular animated television series Slugterra.

Collect little critters called slugs then fire them out of your high-powered blaster, transforming them into magical battle beasts!

MY ROLES

.

Level Design - Designed multiple levels, enemies
and slugs throughout the game.

Game Design - Designed multiple chapters, new minigames
and gameplay types for bonus levels. Tuned and
balanced progression difficulty for every level.
Balance and tune core gameplay mechanics.

Scripting - Used Objective C and .XML for recreating the gameplay. Included everything from simple levels to slugs to entirely new game modes.

Art and Sound implementation - Responsible for adding all art, animations, music and sound effects
