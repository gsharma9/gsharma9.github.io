GLENROSE VISION - Game Design and Game Programmer

.

SUMMARY

.

Glenrose Vision is a mixed reality first-person shooter, where space creatures have invaded your space. Use air tap and gaze gestures to target and shoot down enemies. Players can dodge incoming fire in real-time.

The goal of this project is to challenge patients that suffer from visual challenges or reaction time challenges in rehabilitation. By using this game, patients scan large portions of their field of view, as well as work on the “lighthouse” method of scanning.

Therapists will be able to use the system to modify and play the game on the HoloLens for their patients, allowing them a more streamlined workflow.
