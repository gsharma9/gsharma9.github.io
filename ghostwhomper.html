GHOST WHOMPER - Level Designer

.

SUMMARY

.

The stump is overrun with ghosts! Play as Pickle, Merl or Gull, and grab your ghost whomper to rid the stump of its spooky guests.

Use the mouse to shine your flashlight, and whomp the items in each room to shake any ghosts out of hiding.

Collect AA batteries to keep your ghost catching gadgets juiced. If your flashlight runs out of power, it’s game over, because it’s way too scary to chase ghosts in the dark!

MY ROLES

.

Level Design - Designed multiple levels and
ghosts within the areas to fit the gameplay scenarios.

Game Design - Designed multiple new minigames
and gameplay types to find ghosts. Tuned and
balanced battling a ghost. Helped balance and tune core gameplay mechanics.

Scripting - Used Unity for scripting to create
the gameplay. Included everything from simple
ghost mechanics, puzzles, art implementation to entering new rooms.
